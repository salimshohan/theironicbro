Classrooms are supposed to be safe spaces. We sit, we listen (mostly), and we wait for the bell.
But sometimes, teachers wake up and choose chaos.

They walk in with a specific look in their eyes. They don't say "Good Morning."
They say the six scariest words in the English language:
**"Put your books away. Surprise Quiz."**

My soul leaves my body. My stomach drops to the floor. My future flashes before my eyes.
Here is the anatomy of a Pop Quiz disaster.
