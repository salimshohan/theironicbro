There was a time when Math was beautiful.
1 + 1 = 2.
5 x 5 = 25.
It made sense. It was logical. We were happy.

But then, something terrible happened. The numbers started disappearing.
They were replaced by letters. Then by Greek symbols. Then by shapes that look like squashed snakes.

Now, I sit in Math class not calculating numbers, but translating an ancient alien language.

Here is the breakdown of my daily struggle in the war against Calculus.

If you look away for even 5 seconds, you are doomed.

* **09:00 AM:** The teacher writes `2x + y = 10`. Okay, I get this. Easy.
* **09:00:05 AM:** I drop my pen. I bend down to pick it up.

**Me:** "What happened? Did I travel forward in time? How did we get here?"
