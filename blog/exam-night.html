Exam Night Realizations: When the Syllabus is an Ocean

It is currently 2:00 AM. The fan is rotating at full speed, but I am still sweating. Why? Because I just calculated that to finish my syllabus, I need to study 4 chapters per hour. Is that mathematically possible? Probably.

Is it humanly possible? Nah Bro.

The 3 Stages of Grief (Exam Edition)

1. Denial: "It won't take long. I just need to read the summary."
2. Panic: Opening the book and realizing the 'summary' is 50 pages long.
3. Acceptance: Looking for the 'Top 10 Important Questions' on YouTube and praying for a miracle.

Every student knows the pain. You open the book, and suddenly the wall looks interesting. The ceiling fan pattern looks fascinating. Even the wrapper of a biscuit looks like art.

The Solution?

There is no solution. We just drink coffee, stay awake, and promise ourselves: "Next semester, I will study from day one." (We know that's a lie).
