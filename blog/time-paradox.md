Time is relative. Einstein said it, but students *live* it.
When you are in a boring lecture, 5 minutes feels like 5 years.
When you have an assignment due, 5 days feels like 5 seconds.

We all suffer from a chronic condition called **"Future Me Syndrome."**
We believe that "Future Me" will be smarter, faster, and more energetic.
Spoiler Alert: "Future Me" is just as lazy as "Present Me," but with more panic.

Here is the lifecycle of the Time Paradox.
