But the moment water falls from the sky? **System Failure.**

My brain interprets a light drizzle not as "weather," but as a "National Emergency."
* **Reality:** It is raining slightly. You might need an umbrella.
* **My Brain:** "THIS IS THE TITANIC. THE ROADS ARE RIVERS. STAY IN BED OR YOU WILL PERISH."
