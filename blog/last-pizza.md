There is no tension in the world greater than the moment a pizza box is opened, devoured, and then... **one single slice remains.**

It sits there in the center of the grease-stained cardboard. The cheese is glistening. The pepperoni is calling your name.
But you cannot just take it. Oh no. That would be rude. That would be savage.

Instead, we engage in a psychological battle called **"The Polite Standoff."**
It is a game of chess, but with mozzarella.

Here is how the war unfolds.

* **Friend 2:** "Yeah, me too. Someone else take it." (Lie. He is visually measuring the pepperoni).
