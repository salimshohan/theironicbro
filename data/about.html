We Turn Pain Into Memes

Because therapy is expensive, but sarcasm is free. Welcome to the official headquarters of relatable struggles.

How It Started

It was a dark and stormy night (actually, it was just a humid Tuesday in Rajshahi). I had an exam the next day. The syllabus was huge. My motivation was zero.


Instead of studying, I opened Photoshop. I made a meme about how doomed I was. My friends laughed. I failed the exam, but I won the internet.


Thus, The Ironic Bro was born. We are not a corporate brand. We are just a group of students trying to survive academic trauma through humor.
