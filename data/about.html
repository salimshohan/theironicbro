The Origin Story

It wasn't a planned startup. It wasn't a million-dollar idea discussed in a boardroom. It started in a messy room in Rajshahi, at 3:00 AM, the night before a Physics exam.

I was staring at my textbook, trying to understand thermodynamics, but my brain was playing "Moye Moye" on a loop. I realized I had two choices: actually study and pass the exam, or make a meme about how I'm going to fail. Naturally, I chose the meme.

That one meme got a few likes from my friends. Then I made another. And another. I realized that my pain was not just mine—it was universal. Every student was fighting the same battle against syllabus, alarm clocks, and annoying relatives.

So, The Ironic Bro was born. I built this website not because I am a coding genius, but because I wanted a place to store my sarcasm permanently. I learned HTML and CSS just to avoid learning Calculus. And honestly? It was worth it.

Today, this isn't just a page. It's a sanctuary for every backbencher, every last-minute studier, and everyone who believes that laughter is the best way to survive academic trauma.
